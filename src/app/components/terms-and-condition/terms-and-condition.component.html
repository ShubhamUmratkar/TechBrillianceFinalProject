Tech Brilliance Academy - Terms and Conditions for 100% Job Guarantee
1. Introduction

Tech Brilliance Academy is committed to providing students with comprehensive education and skill development. Our 100% Job Guarantee is offered to students who successfully complete the specified courses under the following terms and conditions.

2. Eligibility Criteria

To qualify for the 100% Job Guarantee, the student must:

  • Successfully complete the course with the minimum required grades or performance metrics.
  • Attend and complete all assessments, assignments, and projects as part of the course curriculum.
  • Maintain a minimum attendance of 90% throughout the course duration.
  • Actively participate in all career guidance and placement preparation sessions provided by the Academy.
  • Be open to relocation and flexible regarding job location, if required.
3. Job Placement Process
  • Upon successful course completion, the Academy will assist in job placement within 6 months of the course completion date.
  • The placement opportunities will be sourced from the Academy's network and partnership with OpenFuture Technologies Private Limited and other associated organizations.
  • Students are required to attend interviews and placement drives organized by the Academy. Refusal to attend or participate in these events will void the job guarantee.
